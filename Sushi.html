Sushi

Description

Impress your guests by making homemade sushi.

Or better, host a make-your-own-sushi party at your house.

The choice of fillings is entirely up to you!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Sushi Rice (14 ounces/400 grams)
  • 7 ounces Smoked Salmon (200 grams)
  • 1 Large Ripe Avocado
  • 1 Small Cucumber
  • 1 Small Carrot (optional)
